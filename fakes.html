Improve Your Follower Ratio!

  1. Go to "Settings and activity" on Instagram
  2. Click on "Your activity"
  3. Click on "Download your information" then "Download or transfer information"
  4. Click on the account you want data from then click "Next"
  5. Click on "Some of your information"
  6. Check the "Followers and following" box then click "Next"
  7. Click on the following options:
    • Date range: All time (or lower if you've had Instagram for less than 3 years)
    • Notify: Enter an email (where you will be notified to download your info)
    • Format: JSON
    • Media quality: Medium
  8. Click "Create files" and wait a few minutes to receive an email
  9. Download the files using the instructions in the email
  10. Upload the files below
